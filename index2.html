M&W Camp is a well-known international organization who focused on helping female students from developing countries to study in Scotland. We have already helped 3,200 females from 25 developing countries in South Africa, South America and Asian countries during last 10 years. We also provide major consultation help, financial support and internship opportunities for these students. If you want to know more details about our project, click on history.
